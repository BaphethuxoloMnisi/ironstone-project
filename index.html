About Ironstone Group

Company Overview

South Africa's Multi-Disciplinary Partner

Ironstone Group (Pty) Ltd is a diversified South African company operating within the construction, supply, maintenance, and industrial services sector. Structured to deliver reliable, cost-effective, and quality-driven solutions, we serve both public and private sector clients.

We position ourselves as a multi-disciplinary service provider capable of supporting infrastructure development, facilities maintenance, supply chain solutions, and technical support services with full compliance and operational efficiency.

Ironstone Group is committed to delivering excellence through professionalism, compliance, and a scalable operational model that adapts to the diverse needs of our clients.

Construction & Infrastructure Services

We deliver civil and building construction services tailored for government and private sector projects of all scales. Our teams are equipped for site development, structural works, roadworks, and full building construction from foundation to finish.

With an OHS-compliant workforce and access to machinery, equipment networks, and skilled labour, Ironstone Group handles your infrastructure needs from start to completion.

  • Roadworks & site preparation

Core Service

Supply of Goods & Materials

Ironstone Group supplies a comprehensive range of construction, industrial, and office-related commodities. Our CSD-aligned supply capabilities enable seamless procurement for government departments and private clients.

From raw building materials to IT equipment, we manage sourcing, procurement, and delivery with reliability and competitive pricing.

  • Hand tools & construction equipment
  • Heavy machinery & hydraulic systems
  • Cement, concrete, steel components
  • Plumbing & electrical materials
  • Doors, windows, glass & insulation
  • Office equipment & stationery
  • IT equipment & accessories
  • Furniture (commercial & institutional)
  • PPE & safety gear

Core Service

Facilities Management & Maintenance

Keeping your facilities running at peak performance is what we do. Ironstone Group provides professional building maintenance, cleaning, waste management, and decontamination services for institutional and commercial environments.

Our facilities teams are trained, equipped, and supervised to meet the highest cleanliness and safety standards for government buildings, schools, offices, and industrial spaces.

  • Building maintenance & repair
  • Cleaning & janitorial services
  • Waste management & disposal
  • Decontamination & environmental cleaning
  • Industrial laundry services
  • Support services provision
